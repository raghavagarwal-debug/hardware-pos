# рҹ“– About

**ShopSphere ERP** is a modern **cloud-based Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP)** platform built to simplify inventory management, billing, customer management, reporting, and daily business operations for retailers, wholesalers, and small businesses.

Originally developed for a real client, ShopSphere ERP has grown beyond a custom business solution into the foundation of a larger visionвҖ”to build a scalable **Software as a Service (SaaS)** platform capable of serving businesses across multiple industries.

The project focuses on combining simplicity, security, and scalability while providing an intuitive experience for business owners.
